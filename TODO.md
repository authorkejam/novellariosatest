## URL Pattern:
- `/` - Halaman utama (daftar novel)
- `/novels` - Daftar Novels
- `/novels/[id]` - Detail novel (daftar chapter)
- `/novels/[id]/chapter/[chapterIndex]` - Pembaca chapter
- `/blogs` - Daftar Blogs
- `/novella` - Daftar Novella
- `/about` - Halaman About
